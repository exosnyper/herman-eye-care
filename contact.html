Visit Us at Herman Eye Care in The Pas

Herman Eye Care provides eye exams and also dispenses eyeglasses, contact lenses and sunglasses. If you are interested in learning more about our products and services, or if you would like to schedule an appointment, please feel free to give us a call.

If you are visiting our office, we are located on Highway 10 across from the RCMP station.

Parking is offered on the east side of the building and in the back.

For emergencies, please go to The Pas Hospital
